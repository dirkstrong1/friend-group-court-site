The rule

Real friends make the call. No public drama feed. No AI pretending to be an unbiased judge.

A complete case in three moves.

Built for roommates, parties, trips, and group chats—the kind of disputes that deserve a dramatic ruling, not a serious intervention.

File both sides

State the harmless dispute and give each friend room to make their case.

Invite the jury

Share an unlisted link directly with the group. Jurors vote without exposing individual ballots.

Render the verdict

Reveal the aggregate result and a shareable ruling for the group chat.

Keep it petty.

Friend Group Court is entertainment only. It is not for threats, abuse, crimes, minors, private information, emergencies, or legal, medical, and mental-health decisions. Serious situations belong outside the app with qualified help.

Friend Group Court is preparing a private beta. The app, cloud service, support inbox, and public policies are not yet live.
